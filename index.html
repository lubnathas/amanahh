Repair

Chiller Maintenance

Chiller Maintenance

We provide preventive and corrective maintenance for air cooled and water cooled chiller systems to ensure reliable operation and efficient cooling. Our programs focus on early issue detection, reduced breakdowns, and consistent performance in commercial and industrial facilities.

Our technicians inspect compressors, evaporators, condensers, and control panels, including refrigerant checks, leak detection, electrical testing, control calibration, and safety interlock verification to ensure safe and stable operation.

Through regular servicing and performance optimization, we help improve efficiency, reduce power consumption, and extend equipment life. Emergency breakdown support and annual maintenance contracts ensure uninterrupted operation and long term reliability.

Servicing

AHU & FCU Services

AHU & FCU Services

We provide complete servicing of Air Handling Units and Fan Coil Units to ensure efficient airflow, stable cooling, and consistent indoor comfort across residential, commercial, and industrial facilities.

Our services include filter cleaning or replacement, coil chemical cleaning, and inspection of fans, motors, belts, and bearings. We also service chilled water valves, actuators, and thermostats to maintain accurate temperature control and smooth operation.

We perform airflow balancing and rectify noise or vibration issues while improving indoor air quality and system efficiency. Our approach helps extend equipment life and maintain a comfortable, healthy indoor environment.

Maintenance

Package Units

Package Units

We provide preventive and corrective maintenance services for packaged HVAC units to ensure reliable operation and consistent cooling performance. Our approach focuses on early fault detection, efficient servicing, and quick restoration of system functionality to reduce downtime.

Our technicians perform compressor health checks, refrigerant system inspections, and thorough cleaning of condenser and evaporator coils. We also inspect electrical panels, troubleshoot control issues, and verify duct connections and airflow performance to ensure safe and efficient system operation.

With quick response repair services and planned maintenance support, we help extend equipment life, improve energy efficiency, and maintain uninterrupted comfort. Our services are designed to keep packaged HVAC systems operating smoothly across residential, commercial, and industrial facilities.

Solutions

Dehumidifiers

Dehumidifiers

We provide customized dehumidifier solutions based on site conditions and specific humidity control requirements. Our systems are suitable for villas, commercial buildings, basements, swimming pools, warehouses, and industrial areas, helping maintain comfortable and healthy indoor environments.

We supply high quality, energy efficient dehumidifiers from approved manufacturers and handle complete installation works. This includes equipment mounting, electrical connections, drainage arrangements, and control integration, ensuring reliable and efficient system operation.

Our dehumidifier systems help improve indoor air quality by controlling moisture, mold, and condensation. They can be integrated with existing AHU, FCU, and ventilation systems, with testing, commissioning, and after sales maintenance support available for long term performance.
